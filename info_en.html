Number of days in Macau

Hong Kong and Macau Pass, Chinese passport for transit through Macau (do not lose the entry receipt! It is used to prove the valid stay date)
Chinese Passport A 7-day stay is granted upon first entry; if you do not go to other countries through Macau Airport, a 2-day stay is granted upon second entry within 30 days of departure
Entering Macao with Hong Kong and Macao Pass The Hong Kong and Macau Pass grants a 7-day stay permit to enter Macau
