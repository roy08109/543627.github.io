FAQ

How to book a Macau sauna? ▼
Contact us via WeChat (Allinmacau) or LINE, tell us your group size and preferences, and we'll arrange a free shuttle service.
Does the price include all costs? ▼
Some venues charge an additional 10% government tax. Booking through us gives you an $800 discount, making it cheaper than walk-in prices.
Is there free shuttle service? ▼
Yes! We provide free shuttle service across Macau, from any location (hotel, border, casino) directly to the sauna venue.
Can I pay by credit card? ▼
Most venues accept credit cards and WeChat Pay. Some only accept cash (HKD/MOP). Contact us in advance to confirm.
What are the business hours? ▼
Most Macau saunas operate daily from 3:00 PM to 5:00 AM. Some venues offer 24-hour service.
